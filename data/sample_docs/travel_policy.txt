Corporate Travel Policy

Purpose:
This policy defines reimbursement rules for business travel.

Air Travel:
Employees should book economy class for domestic trips.
Business class requires vice president approval for international travel longer than eight hours.

Hotel Reimbursement:
Hotel reimbursement is allowed up to 200 USD per night for standard travel locations.
Higher rates require pre-approval from the travel coordinator.

Meals and Incidentals:
Meal reimbursement is capped at 60 USD per day.
Receipts are required for any individual expense above 25 USD.

Ground Transportation:
Ride-share, taxi, rail, and airport shuttle services are reimbursable when used for business purposes.
