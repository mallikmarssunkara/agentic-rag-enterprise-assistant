Employee Handbook Excerpt

Working Hours:
Regular office hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM from Monday through Friday.
Employees may request flexible scheduling with manager approval.

Remote Work:
Eligible employees may work remotely up to two days per week.
Remote work requests must be approved by the employee's manager and documented with Human Resources.

Leave Policy:
Full-time employees receive 15 days of paid time off each calendar year.
Unused paid time off may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days.

Code of Conduct:
Employees are expected to maintain professional behavior, protect confidential information, and comply with company technology policies.
