IT Security Policy Summary

Password Requirements:
Passwords must contain at least 12 characters and include a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

Multi-Factor Authentication:
All employees must use multi-factor authentication when accessing cloud applications and remote systems.

Incident Reporting:
Suspected phishing emails or data security incidents must be reported to the IT security team within one hour of discovery.

Device Usage:
Only company-managed devices may be used to access internal business systems unless an exception is approved in writing.
